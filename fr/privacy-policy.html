You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data that we base on our legitimate interest, for reasons relating to your personal situation. You may also opt out of personalized advertising based on your profile. To learn more, see Sections 5 and 6.

However, please note that even if you object to certain processing, TIMING CORPORATION may continue such processing if it is based on another legal basis. For example, to provide you with the Game (if you still wish to use it) or to comply with legal obligations.

Right to restrict

You may request that the use of certain of your personal data be restricted, for example if the personal data is inaccurate, if you consider that TIMING CORPORATION no longer needs the personal data for the purposes described in this Policy, or if you have objected to the use of your personal data based on legitimate interest.

We may also ask you to specify the information you wish to access.

You also have the right, at any time, to request the correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)

You may request that we erase your personal data, for example if you have deleted your account and the personal data is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, if you withdraw your consent and there is no other legal basis for processing the data, or if you object to our use of your data based on legitimate interest. Right to withdraw consent

If you have given your consent to TIMING CORPORATION for the processing of your personal data, you may always withdraw such consent to prevent any future processing based on that consent.

Right to data portability

You have the right to receive personal data concerning you, which you have provided to us and which we use on the basis of your consent or to perform a contract with you, in a commonly used and readable format, and to have this information transmitted to another platform where technically feasible.

6. Advertising identifiers

Advertising identifiers are identifiers that devices, such as mobile phones, provide to identify that device to advertising partners. This identifier is used by advertisers to serve more relevant personalized advertisements in applications and Games.

You can opt out of personalized advertising on your device by disabling your device’s advertising identifier. The exact procedure depends on the device you use, but generally you can change this setting in the privacy section of your device preferences. Please note that you will still see advertisements if you disable the advertising identifier, but they will not be based on your profile and will likely be less relevant to you.
